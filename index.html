Soundcloud Stream Manager

Cut out the garbage from your listening with the Soundcloud Stream Manager, modding your Soundcloud experience to make consumption easier. This project has been in various guises since Soundcloud removed pagination from the stream and turned into a big bloated mess. This is designed to make things smoother.

How to better manage your Soundcloud stream

This bookmarklet enables you to do some cool things, like:

  • Remove Reposts from your stream
  • Remove tracks over a certain age (for pruning Repost Masturbators)
  • Remove tracks over a certain length for hiding mixes
  • Remove tracks before the one your looking at or listening to, to help with browser performace
  1. Drag this link to your Bookmark Bar Soundcloud Stream Manager
  2. Go to your Soundcloud Stream
  3. Click the Bookmarket. A panel will appear in the top-righthand corner of your browser
  4. Start clicking buttons
  5. Optional: Follow me on Twitter and Mixcloud as a thank you

The nuts and bolts of how the script works is Githubbed, so you can see it there if you care.
